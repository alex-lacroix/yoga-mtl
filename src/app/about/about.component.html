About Us

Tanya Dawe, Founder of Corporate Yoga Montreal, is a yoga and meditation teacher with a passion for health and wellness. She has been practicing yoga for nearly 20 years and has been teaching since 2012. She had a decade-long career in nursing and pharmaceuticals but decided to embark on a major lifestyle change in 2015 by opening her yoga studio, Yoga Vieux Montreal. She loves to use her yoga knowledge and personal experiences in the corporate setting to help companies bring wellness and balance to their employees. Tanya has studied yoga and meditation in Indonesia, Hawaii, India and Montreal.

We also have a network of highly trained corporate yoga teachers on our team and will work with you to find the teacher who perfectly fits your needs.
